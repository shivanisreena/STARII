Mission Overview

Objective

STARI demonstrates space-based optical interferometry using two 6U CubeSats flying in precise formation. Its goals are to prove starlight handoff between satellites, validate sub-centimeter relative navigation, and raise the readiness of key optical and propulsion subsystems for future exoplanet-imaging missions.

Read more

Core Technologies

Each CubeSat carries a compact optical payload with dual fast-steering mirrors, high-speed cameras, and LED beacon tracking. Sensor-fusion GNC and cold-gas micro-propulsion enable millimeter-level control, while cross-link telemetry supports synchronized operation and data exchange.
